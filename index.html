Endorsements

“Finally there is a resource that combines the four Gospels into one continuous and compelling account that makes the story of Jesus even more accessible to those who seek Him. Additionally, Old Testament prophecies that foretell Christ’s words and deeds are inserted in a way that enriches the whole and everything is color coded for easy identification. I commend John Burkhalter for his labor intensive effort that promises much reward for those who engage it.”
– Tony Perkins, President, Family Research Council, Washington, DC.

“More than a decade in the making, this work melds the four eyewitness accounts of Jesus’ life into a single version to provide a concise, unified perspective. I highly encourage laymen, pastors and teachers to take this presentation seriously as a valuable learning document for those who wish to understand Jesus’ complete teachings.
“Conceivably, this book could be problematic for some scholars and Bible theologians. However, it does not have to be lost in endless debates over the less important points of precise chronology and matters of minutiae. Burkhalter has concentrated instead on the more weighty matters of exacting the full content of the Gospels so that nothing is lost.
“Also conceived and formatted as a movie script, this document is a significant work with imaginative potential. It could be the basis for a landmark mini-series of film or TV. It’s clearly useful as a text book of the gospels for Bible schools and seminaries and is a treasure for the church for use in small group Bible Studies, church plays, etc..
“Jesus, Every Word and Deed” simply offers something we have not seen before.”
– Derrel Emmerson, Ordained Pastor (retired); Christian Assembly, Vienna, VA; Minister of The United Methodist Church; Chaplain, US Army; Professor of Pastoral Ministry of Evangel Seminary, Harrisonburg, VA.

“This work of unifying the Gospel accounts into one cohesive narrative and then adding, at the appropriate points, many of the Old Testament Prophesies that anticipate and foretell the very words and deeds of our Savior is remarkable. You must read this to truly appreciate the attention to detail that helps the most important life of all time to come alive through the written word.”
– Pastor Jay Miller, M.Div., Erskine Theological Seminary, Sr. Pastor, Parkway Presbyterian Church, Cumming, GA

“What a blessing to hear God’s WORD like it’s happening right now! The introduction sums up 1300 years of history to make the listener ready for the revelation of the proclamation of the birth of the Savior of the world!”

“Burkhalter has painstakingly poured over the Scriptures—not only to present us with every word and deed of Jesus, but also to show us how the law and the prophets so clearly point to the Messiah’s fulfillment of the promises of God. If you want to encounter Jesus, open this book.”
– Matthew Bullard, B.A. Biblical Studies, M.A. Christian Education Director of Christian Education, Parkway Presbyterian Church

“Have you ever finished reading the four gospels and thought “I wish someone would weave all four together so it would read as a complete story as if by a single author”? Look no farther. Jesus’ Every Word and Deed does that for you using nothing but the scripture wordings to tell the story.”
